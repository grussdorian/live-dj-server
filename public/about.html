Dating, But Without the Creepy Overlords

Welcome to Fheya - the dating app for people who value privacy more than their ex's apology text.

Why Fheya?

Because swiping right on a soulmate shouldn't mean swiping left on your privacy. Unlike other apps (we're looking at you, Tinder), we don't sell your data, judge your life choices, or let algorithms decide your love life. We just help you find someone to split the bill with.

Our Values

  1. No Data, No Drama
    • We don't store your secrets. We don't sell your secrets. We don't even know your secrets. Your data stays yours—encrypted, locked, and thrown away like last year's New Year's resolutions.
  2. Matches That Actually Mean Something
    • One or two real matches per week. No endless swiping, no ghosting Olympics, no "Oops, I super-liked your cat by accident."
  3. No Paywalls, No Games
    • We won't make you pay to see who likes you. That's just emotional blackmail, and we're better than that.
  4. Fairness > Algorithms
    • Our matching isn't rigged by who pays the most. It's random, fair, and based on actual compatibility (not just who swiped right on a blurry bathroom selfie).
  5. QR Codes > Small Talk
    • Verify matches in person. Scan a QR code, meet IRL, and earn brownie points (yes, like cookies, but for not being a catfish).
  6. Privacy So Tight, Even We Can't Peek
    • Your location? Encrypted. Your chats? Encrypted. Your embarrassing profile? Also encrypted. We use Fully Homomorphic Encryption—fancy tech that means we never see your data. Ever.
  7. No Paradox of Choice, Just Good Vibes
    • Women: No more drowning in "Hey" messages. Men: No more wondering if your profile is in the "shadowban" purgatory.
  8. Brownie Points for Being a Decent Human
    • Rate your dates (good or bad). More brownies = better matches. It's like karma, but with math.
  9. Matches Expire - Like Milk, But Less Gross
    • A week to chat, meet, or ghost. Then, poof - it's gone. No digital clutter, no regrets.

This is a lightweight song request app used to collect and manage Spotify track requests for live DJ sessions.

How it works

Visitors paste a Spotify track link or ID, which is validated and queued. Admins may approve requests to move them into the live playlist. The app can optionally mirror playlists to Spotify and persist state in Redis.
